French Toast

Ingrediants:

• 1 Egg

• 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

• 1/2 Teaspoon cinnamon

• 1/4 cup milk

• 2 bread slices

Instructions:

• Toast your 2 pieces of bread

• Mix all your ingrediants into your egg mixture

• Dip your toast into your egg mixture

• Put your toast on the stove and cook each side for 3mins.
